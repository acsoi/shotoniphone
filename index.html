Photography is everywhere.
Great photography
takes curation.

Shot on iPhone Club exists because the most impressive camera in the world is already in your pocket — and the people using it deserve a proper stage, and a fair cut.

01 — Origin

Born from the campaign, built for the community.

Apple's Shot on iPhone campaign proved something important: the line between amateur and professional photography had dissolved. Billions of extraordinary images were being taken daily, most disappearing into camera rolls. We built this club to give those images — and their creators — the recognition they deserve.

02 — Curation

Not everything. The best things.

We review every submission. Our curatorial team looks for photographs that stop you mid-scroll — images with genuine emotion, unexpected light, or a composition that couldn't have been planned. We accept less than 8% of submissions. That selectivity is what makes membership meaningful.

03 — Commerce

Downloads first. Prints when they're earned.

Digital downloads are the core of what we sell — instant, high-margin, and you keep 60%. Prints are reserved for the monthly showcase: the top 3 most downloaded shots become limited, numbered runs. The community decides through downloads. No gatekeeping. Your cut splits via Stripe Connect at the moment of every sale.

04 — Infrastructure

Payments built properly from day one.

Digital downloads run through Paddle — they act as Merchant of Record, meaning every VAT rate, sales tax, and global compliance requirement is handled automatically. Physical print orders split via Stripe Connect the moment a sale happens. You never chase a payout.

05 — Community

A club, not a platform.

Platforms extract. Clubs belong to their members. Every design decision, every policy, every feature — made with the photographer's interest at the centre. We grow when our members grow.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Who we are

Shot on iPhone Club operates at shotoniphone.club. We are an independent photography community and are not affiliated with Apple Inc. in any way.

What data we collect

We collect the following only when you actively provide it:

—Name and email address (when you submit a photo or sign up)
—Instagram handle (optional, for attribution)
—Stripe Connect details (handled entirely by Stripe — we never see banking information)
—Public Instagram post ID and handle (if discovered via #ShotOniPhone — held only until curation decision)
—Consent status (your response to our outreach)
—Purchase history (processed via Paddle or Stripe)

Instagram Graph API

We use the Instagram Graph API (Meta Platforms, Inc.) to discover public posts tagged #ShotOniPhone. We access only public content. Post IDs and handles are held temporarily during curation and deleted if we decide not to reach out. We do not store or republish any Instagram data without explicit consent.

Third-party processors

—Paddle — digital download payments and global VAT/sales tax as Merchant of Record
—Stripe — membership and print payments; Stripe Connect for photographer payouts
—Gelato — print fulfilment; receives shipping address for delivery only
—Manychat — Instagram DM consent flows (Meta Business Partner)

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing privacy@shotoniphone.club. We will respond within 30 days. UK and EU residents have additional rights under UK GDPR / GDPR including rights to data portability and to object to processing.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies necessary to operate the site. No tracking, advertising, or third-party analytics cookies.
